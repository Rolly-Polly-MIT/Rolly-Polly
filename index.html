Rolly Polly

Not all eligible citizens of the United States have access to voting resources. Rolly Polly can change that.

Let's Vote!


In America, all adult citizens should have the right to vote. However, not all eligible voters are able to, including some people actively being obstructed from exercising their constitutional right. The political voices of already underrepresented minorities are silenced, which is in violation of their rights and a threat to our overall democracy. Rolly Polly attempts to give users an easy and logical way to view and understand their local laws, resources, and rights regarding voting, encourages users to register to vote, and gives insights to regions in the United States experiencing the most voter suppression.
